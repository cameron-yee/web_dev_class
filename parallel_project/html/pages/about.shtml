In 2016, Cameron Yee begin his journey as a mountain biker. He started off riding trails near the towns of Lyons and Boulder in northern Colorado. After moving to Colorado Springs, Cameron began shredding trails in the southern Colorado area. After some time of maintaining mountain biking as a hobby, Cameron decided to make his passion a full time job. On September 4, 2017, Cameron opened Cam Bam's Bike Shop in beautiful Santa Cruz, Colorado. Today, if you can always find Cameron either working on bikes in the shop or out shredding the local trails.

Mission Statement: Cam Bam's Bike Shop is dedicated to being each bicyclist's personal team that they can count on for anything mountain bike related. Vision Statement: We will strive to show all bikers the glory of mountain biking.

Custom Geometry: A bike should be a perfect fit for its rider. That is why we highly recommend building bike frames to fit each specific rider. Custom geometry allows a rider to optimize each aspect of their bike for their specific needs and wants.
